FAQ
Questions people actually ask.
What actually is Sat Space?
A limited, 2 month run of ad space in all my reels once Sat Space goes live. 144 total slots, priced in sats, first come first served.
Why is this priced in sats instead of euros?
Let me be honest, I'd love to just take sats straight up. But here in Austria that's a pain in the ass. Invoicing in euros, paying the regular tax on that, then converting what's left into sats myself and sending it straight into the current challenge, that's the clean way to do it. So sats set the price. Euros settle the bill.
What if Bitcoin's price moves after I pay?
Here's the fun part. If Bitcoin's price goes up before you check out, your slot costs you a bit more in euros. If it drops, you get it cheaper. Either way, you're always paying the exact same amount of Bitcoin. Kind of poetic for a Bitcoin challenge, no?
What content is allowed?
Any static image works as your slot's artwork. Be creative, promote your company, slap your face on it if you like. But there are rules. No adult content, no hate, no violence, no scam promotion. And let's not get political on this one. Every submission gets a manual approval pass by me before it goes live in an actual reel.
What happens once the 2 months are up?
Every slot disappears on the same end date, no matter when you bought in. That's also the reason to buy early, earlier buyers get more total airtime out of the same fixed window.
