Datenschutzerklärung

The protection of your personal data is important to us. We process your data only on the basis of applicable law (GDPR, Austrian TKG 2003). This page explains the key aspects of how data is handled on this website.

Contact with us

This website does not have a contact form. If you contact us by email, the information you provide is stored to process your inquiry and any follow-up questions. We do not share this data without your consent.

Hosting

This website is hosted by Cloudflare, Inc., 101 Townsend St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA, via Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process personal data (such as IP addresses) as part of delivering the site and its backend functions, and provides Standard Contractual Clauses for data transferred outside the EU/EEA. More information: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

Payments

When you purchase a Sat Space slot, payment is processed by Stripe (Stripe Payments Europe, Limited, 1 Grand Canal Street Lower, Dublin 2, Ireland, for EU customers). Stripe collects your payment details, such as card information and billing email, directly. We do not see or store your full card details ourselves. More information: stripe.com/privacy

Database and file storage

Purchase records and uploaded artwork are stored using Supabase, hosted in an EU data center (Frankfurt, Germany). Supabase Inc. is a US-based company, so data may in principle remain subject to US legal requests even while stored on EU servers. More information: supabase.com/privacy

Purchase notifications

When a purchase is completed, a notification with basic purchase details, which may include your email address, is sent to a private Discord channel so it can be reviewed promptly. This is provided by Discord Inc., 444 De Haro Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA. More information: discord.com/privacy

Cookies

This website does not currently use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts.

Data retention

Purchase and payment records are kept for as long as required under Austrian tax and accounting law. Exact retention periods are being confirmed with a tax advisor.

Your rights

You generally have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and objection regarding your personal data. If you believe processing of your data violates data protection law, you can file a complaint with the supervisory authority. In Austria, this is the Datenschutzbehörde (dsb.gv.at).
